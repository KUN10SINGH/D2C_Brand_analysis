# D2C_Brand_analysis
Customer Retention and Value Analytics Using Python, SQL, and Power BI

This project focused on understanding customer behavior, loyalty, and value generation through a data-driven analytics framework. Using a customer-level retail dataset, the objective was to identify key drivers of customer retention, develop customer segmentation strategies, and generate actionable business recommendations to improve long-term profitability.

The project began with data cleaning and preprocessing, where records containing missing review ratings were removed to ensure data quality. Since the dataset represented customer profiles rather than individual transactions, feature engineering was performed to create meaningful business metrics. A Loyalty Score was developed using Previous Purchases, Frequency of Purchases, Review Rating, and Subscription Status to measure customer engagement and retention potential. A Customer Value Score was also created to estimate the relative business value contributed by each customer.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was conducted to examine demographic, behavioral, and purchasing patterns. Statistical techniques including correlation analysis, hypothesis testing, t-tests, ANOVA, and Chi-Square tests were applied to validate business assumptions and identify significant relationships among customer attributes. Customers were segmented into Champion, Loyal, Regular, and At-Risk groups based on their loyalty characteristics, enabling targeted retention strategies.

The analysis further explored promotional effectiveness, geographic performance, and customer demographics to identify underutilized markets and high-potential customer segments. An Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) was developed by studying the characteristics of customers who exhibited both high loyalty and high value.

SQL was used for data transformation, analytical querying, and creation of reusable views to support business intelligence workflows. Finally, interactive Power BI dashboards were designed to visualize customer loyalty trends, value distribution, segmentation outcomes, promotional insights, and regional performance metrics. The project concluded with a customer retention playbook that provided strategic recommendations for customer engagement, retention improvement, marketing optimization, and data-driven decision-making, helping transform raw customer data into actionable business intelligence.
